Clothing Store Sales Analysis (Excel Project)

One of my projects was a Clothing Store Sales Analysis project, where the goal was to analyze sales performance and identify business insights from a 12-month retail sales dataset.

The dataset had over 50,000 rows and included information like product category, customer orders, revenue, and purchase trends.

My objective was to identify top-performing products, customer buying patterns, and seasonal sales trends that could help improve inventory and sales planning.

To do this, I used Advanced Excel, including Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, and Power Query for data cleaning and analysis. I also used VBA Macros to automate monthly reporting.

I analyzed revenue trends, top-selling SKUs, category-wise performance, and customer purchase behavior. I also created summary reports and dashboards to make the insights easier to understand.

As an outcome, the project helped identify peak sales periods, top revenue-generating products, and demand patterns, which could support better inventory planning and faster business decisions.
